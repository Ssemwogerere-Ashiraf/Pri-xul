About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1985, St. Mary's Primary School has been providing quality education to the children of Uganda for over 38 years. We started with just 50 students and have grown to become one of the most respected primary schools in the region.

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that develops students academically, morally, and socially, preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders of Uganda.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a dynamic world.

Our Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Integrity and honesty
  • Respect for all
  • Community service
  • Lifelong learning

Admissions

Join Our School Family

We welcome applications for all classes from P.1 to P.7. Our admission process is designed to be simple and transparent.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs (2)
  • Medical examination report
  • Transfer letter (for transfers)

Application Process

  1. Submit completed application form
  2. Attend interview with parents/guardians
  3. Placement assessment (for appropriate classes)
  4. Receive admission letter
  5. Complete registration and payment
  6. Begin classes!
